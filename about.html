Why I built this site

After my gastric bypass, I spent weeks trying to figure out which vitamin was right for me. Every site either sold me something or overwhelmed me with information.

In January 2024, I had gastric bypass surgery and lost 105 pounds over the following year. Like every post-op patient, I needed to take bariatric vitamins for life. The problem: there was no clear, independent comparison of the options.

I found sites that reviewed vitamins but sold their own brand. I found clinical PDFs that listed products but gave no prices. I found forum posts from 2018. What I could not find was a clean, current, independent table that answered the one question I had: which vitamin gives me what I need at the lowest cost per pill?

WLS Multi is my answer to that question. I compare every major bariatric multivitamin by price per pill, iron content, B12, daily dose, and surgery type. I update it monthly. I earn a small commission if you buy through my links — at no extra cost to you. That is how I keep the site free.

What I check every month

Every 30 days I verify prices, formulations, and coupon codes for every product in the comparison table.

Current prices

I check each product's direct price and Amazon price and use the lowest.

Iron content

Formulations change. I verify the elemental iron per serving, not just the label claim.

Coupon codes

Every exclusive discount code is tested before the update goes live.

Surgery compatibility

I cross-reference ASMBS guidelines for bypass, sleeve, and mini bypass.
